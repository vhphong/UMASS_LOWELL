Availability of prom file and assembler for RSHIFT, MUL, DIV

There is now a working version of the microcode that implements the 
3 new operations required for Assignment 4 (RSHIFT, MUL, DIV)

You can find these objects at:

~bill/cs305/masm_mrd       (the assembler executable for mercury)
~bill/cs305/prom_mrd.dat   (the microcode needed for RSHIFT, MUL, DIV)
